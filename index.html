mCredit

TBS mCREDIT™ is a out-of-the-box solition, designed for issuing, covering and tracking of mobile credits/loans. mCredit™ is an innovative solution that allows pre-paid subscribers to receive airtime value in credit to extend their talk time once they run out of balance. Once a subscriber runs out of balance, a customized offer is computed for each individual subscriber, and sent instantly via SMS / USSD. If accepted, airtime is immediately credited to the subscriber, who can resume their usage and who will simply pay back when they next top up using their conventional method.

TBS online charging solution

TBS is and event based charging function (EBCF). It is used to charge events based on their occurrence rather than their duration or volume used in the event. Typical events are SMS, MMS, purchase of a content (application, game, music, video on demand, etc.). TBS supports various standard protocols like Diameter, Radius to provide event based charging for mobile customers.

Monitor your revenue with TBS

TBS enables monitoring Billing and Revenue Management targets using mCredit Enterprise Manager Control. A management agent monitors targets for collection items and metrics and sends the data to the management server for presentation.
